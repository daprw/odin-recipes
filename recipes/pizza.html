Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a homemade pizza!

I hope you enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • Raw pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Sliced pepperonis
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Knead the pizza dough into a circle.
  2. Ensure the edge of the dough is slighty raised compared to the centered for crust.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly across the dough.
  4. Spread mozzarella cheese evenly across the dough.
  5. Add sliced pepperonis to the top of the pizza.
  6. Bake pizza at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.
  7. Slice the pizza and enjoy!
