Spaghetti

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious homemade spaghetti dish!

I hope you enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of your choice of meat (ground turkey is used here)
  • Spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Thoroughly cook the pound of meat.
  2. Stir in marinara sauce.
  3. Bring a pot of water to boil.
  4. Add spaghetti noodles to boiling water.
  5. Cook noodles until soft or al dente.
  6. Strain noodles and add to meat sauce.
  7. Serve.
  8. (Optional) - Sprinkle grated parmesan cheese to taste.
